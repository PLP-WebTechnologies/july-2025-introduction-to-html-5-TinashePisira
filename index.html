    Semantic HTML

    Semantic HTML uses tags that convey the meaning of the content, not just its appearance. Tags like `header`, `nav`, `main`, and `footer` are all semantic. This improves both accessibility and SEO.

    -->

    Accessibility (A11Y)

    Accessibility ensures that websites are usable by everyone, including people with disabilities. We use landmark elements, proper heading structure, and descriptive attributes like `aria-label` and `alt` to achieve this.

    On-Page SEO

    On-page SEO refers to optimizing individual web pages to rank higher in search engines. This includes using descriptive titles, meta descriptions, and a clear, hierarchical heading structure to make our content more discoverable.
